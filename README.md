# LOOK – Luxury Eyewear Collection 🕶️

A premium, minimalist e-commerce landing page designed for a high-end eyewear brand. **LOOK** focuses on the intersection of modern aesthetics and artisan craftsmanship, offering a seamless experience for discovering both optical frames and luxury sunglasses.

[![Live Demo](https://img.shields.io/badge/Live-Demo-black)](https://amlalqiran-oss.github.io/eyewear-luxury-collection/)

## ✨ Key Features

- **Minimalist "Redefine Your Vision" Hero:** A clean, high-impact introduction to the Summer 2026 collection.
- **Dual Product Showcases:** Dedicated sections for "New Arrivals" (Optical) and "Sunglasses" featuring high-resolution imagery and pricing.
- **Brand Storytelling:** A "Crafted with Precision" section that highlights the brand's commitment to handcrafted quality and premium materials.
- **Authorized Retailer Gallery:** Showcases partnerships with iconic brands like Ray-Ban, Gucci, Prada, and Oakley.
- **Social Integration:** A curated "Follow Our Journey" Instagram-style lifestyle gallery to drive brand engagement.
- **Responsive Navigation:** A sleek, functional header with a cart indicator and categorized links (Collection, Stories, About).

## 🛠️ Built With

- **HTML5:** Semantic structure focusing on clean content hierarchy.
- **CSS3:** Custom styling for a luxury feel, utilizing modern layouts, refined borders, and elegant spacing.
- **JavaScript:** Powers the interactive navigation and shopping cart UI elements.
- **WebP & Modern Image Formats:** Optimized for high-quality visual performance across devices.
